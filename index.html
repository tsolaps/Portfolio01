Finora

Year2024
TypePersonal Product
RoleDesigner & Developer
StatusIn Development

The Problem

Most financial tools are built for salaried workers with predictable income. In Nigeria, where cash income, informal trading, and irregular pay cycles are the norm, these tools fall flat. People can't track what they can't categorise.

What I Built

A conversational AI finance tool that lets you talk to your money the way you'd talk to a trusted advisor. You describe what happened - "I sold goods worth 40k today, paid 12k for transport" - and Finora handles the categorisation, tracking, and insight.

Mono API integration means Finora can pull real transaction data from your bank and give context-aware responses. No forms. No spreadsheets. Just a conversation.

VoltStore

Year2023
TypePersonal Product
RoleDesigner & Developer
PlatformTelegram

The Problem

Small gadget sellers in Nigeria have customers but no infrastructure. Building a website costs money and time most don't have. Most end up selling through Instagram DMs or WhatsApp - no catalogue, no checkout, no professional experience.

What I Built

An AI-powered Telegram storefront. Sellers list their products once and VoltStore handles everything - customer queries, product recommendations, price negotiations, and order management - through natural conversation.

The negotiation engine was the hardest and most interesting part. It lets buyers haggle within seller-defined limits, giving both sides a human-feeling experience without a human on the other end.

Remidora

Year2023
TypePersonal Product
RoleDesigner & Developer
PlatformTelegram

The Problem

Reminder apps are too rigid. They want you to fill in a date, a time, a title, maybe a category. Nobody thinks like that. You think: "remind me to call the landlord tomorrow morning" or "ping me in 3 hours."

What I Built

A Telegram bot that understands natural language reminders. You type the way you think, and Remidora parses the intent, extracts the time, and fires the reminder when it's due. No forms. No commands. Just a message.

The NLP layer handles relative time ("in 2 hours"), absolute time ("tomorrow at 9am"), and vague language ("soon", "later today") with surprising accuracy.

WhatsApp Automation Suite

Year2024
TypeClient Work
RoleDesigner & Developer
MarketPort Harcourt SMBs

The Problem

Nigerian SMBs run on WhatsApp. Orders, customer service, follow-ups - all happening in the same chat app. But when you're a small team, responding to 200 messages a day while running a business breaks people.

What I Built

A modular AI automation layer that plugs into existing WhatsApp numbers. It handles first-contact responses, order intake, FAQs, and follow-ups - routing to a human only when necessary. Deployed across logistics, gadget retail, and hospitality clients in Port Harcourt.

Active and running for multiple clients. Each deployment is tailored to the business type, with custom flows and response styles.
